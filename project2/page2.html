Nyota's World

Nyota's world is a place where the sun shines and the gentle breeze
carries the scent of blooming flowers.
It's a world that fills her heart with happiness and contentment.

Things I learned from the stars

☁️ That night, I looked up, even the sky has cloudy days, but the stars never really leave✨
I once looked up to others' light, thinking that was the only way to be seen.
I don't need to rush to shine.
💫 My light will find its moment
To shine isn't to be perfect it's to love who I am, right now.
Slowly, I'll glow in my own way 🌟

When I'm feeling down

🌿 No matter how lovely someone else's garden may seem,
the seeds you plant will always be uniquely yours.
You don't need to bloom like anyone else.
Just bloom in your own way 🍀

Cherish spring

🌸 Flowers are unfolding
Not chasing time
Be your own garden
Grow in the sunlight and bloom freely
Always remember to love yourself 💖

You are your own star

🌟 Nyota believes everyone carries a quiet star within 🌟
It doesn't need to be the brightest just bright enough to guide you.
And when doubt finds you, look forward, you've been shining all along✨

When I'm lost in my thoughts

🌀 Slow down, sit in the breeze, and watch the clouds drift by.
Close your eyes, and let your thoughts gently float away with the wind.
Every quiet moment feels like a little gift to yourself 🫂

To live like a plant

🌱 Sprouting in spring
Sleeping through winter ❄️
Waking again the next year
Growing, withering
Growing again, withering again
In an endless cycle
Not rushing anywhere
🎐 Not striving to become anything
Just quietly growing a little every day 🌈

Trying to let go

🍂 Some relationships are like autumn leaves
the tighter you hold onto,
the less graceful the descend.
Sometimes it's better to just gently let go 🌬️

When I'm a bit stressed

Remember to slow down a little, just like the clouds 🌥️
Take a moment to breathe and relax
🐌 Hope your day feels a little softer too

Finding emotional freedom

🗝️ Don't force a smile if it's not there,
don't speak if silence feels right.
Your emotions are not a storefront
they don't need to stay open all the time 📔

Making my way home

🛣️ Some days the road back to yourself feels longer than you remember.
Home isn't always a place you return to
sometimes it's one you slowly build one moment at a time.
🌙 On the long way home
you don't have to arrive all at once.
Come back to yourself slowly,
like light returning after rain 🌤️
